About Me

I am a quantum physicist specializing in optimal quantum control, quantum computation, and large-scale multi-qubit simulations. I have contributed to major UKRI's quantum programs, collaborated with experimental teams, and authored 30 peer-reviewed publications.

  • Programming: Python, MATLAB, Mathematica, Tenpy, QuTiP, Qiskit.
