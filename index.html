About Me

I am a quantum physicist specializing in optimal quantum control, quantum computation, and large-scale multi-qubit simulations. I have contributed to major UKRI's quantum programs, collaborated with experimental teams, authored 30 peer-reviewed publications, and filed two patent applications on quantum computing. I also teach Advanced Quantum Information at Imperial College London.

Research & Expertise

  • Quantum control, quantum computation, and quantum simulation.
  • Trapped ion systems, superconducting qubits, and spin qubits in silicon.
  • Multi-qubit simulations using tensor networks, Lanczos methods, and GPU-accelerated computing.
  • Programming: Python, MATLAB, Mathematica, Tenpy, QuTiP, Qiskit.
